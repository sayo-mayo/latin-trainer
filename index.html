Latin Trainer

Latin declension, conjugation & parsing drills

Latin Trainer is a free drilling tool for Latin morphology. Practise every form the language throws at you: all five declensions, the four conjugations plus mixed, deponent and irregular verbs, participles, infinitives, gerunds and gerundives, pronouns, and adjectives, adverbs and comparison.

  • Produce forms one at a time, or fill in whole paradigm tables graded cell by cell.
  • Parse real inflected forms: case, number, gender, person, tense, mood and voice, including deponents.
  • Translate short sentences and mark yourself against a model answer.
  • Speed runs: race the clock through a full table and climb class and global leaderboards.
  • Track progress with daily accuracy, streaks and per-category counts.

Teachers can create classes with join codes, set scoped assignments with deadlines, see per-student results and attempt history, and find each class's weak spots by form category. Works in any browser; sign in to sync across devices or use it instantly without an account.
